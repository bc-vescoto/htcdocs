Founders Stephen and Margaret de Heinrich de Omorovicza began their journey in the thermal baths of Budapest, where Stephen’s family built the famous Rácz spa in the 19th century. Amazed by the miraculous effect the mineral-rich spa waters had on their skin, the couple enlisted the skills of a Nobel Prize-winning dermatology. With the lab’s expert help, the brand developed its patented Healing Concentrate™ system to deliver therapeutic minerals, resulting in a plumper, younger-looking complexion. Blending these minerals with other natural actives such as purifying Hungarian moor mud, Omorovicza’s products have acquired a cult following worldwide for their anti-aging, glow-giving properties.
